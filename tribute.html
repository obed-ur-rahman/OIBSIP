About Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, also known as the "Missile Man of India", was an Indian scientist and the 11th President of India. He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India. Dr. Kalam made significant contributions to India's space and defense programs, and played a crucial role in the development of ballistic missile technology.

He was a true visionary and a source of inspiration for millions of people, especially the youth. Dr. Kalam's teachings emphasized the importance of education, dreams, and the pursuit of knowledge.
